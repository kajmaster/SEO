# SEO Page Patterns

Use these patterns when `seo-structure` needs a page-specific architecture.

## Service Page

1. H1 with service and buyer situation.
2. Problem and why it matters now.
3. What the service solves.
4. How the approach works.
5. When this is a good fit.
6. Proof, experience, or trust signals.
7. FAQ around process, cost, timing, and suitability.
8. CTA.

## Comparison Page

1. H1 with comparison and decision context.
2. Short explanation of who is comparing and why.
3. Option A: when it makes sense.
4. Option B: when it makes sense.
5. Criteria to compare fairly.
6. Common mistakes in choosing.
7. Recommendation by situation.
8. CTA.

## Local Page

1. H1 with service and location.
2. Local need or situation.
3. Service explanation.
4. Why local context matters.
5. Process or first step.
6. Trust and availability.
7. FAQ about area, timing, and contact.
8. CTA.

For practical local services such as woningontruiming, expand this with:

- concrete triggers: overlijden, verhuizing, zorgopname, verkoop/verhuur, deadline, spoed;
- tasks included: leegruimen, sorteren, afvoeren, schoonmaak, herstel, vloer/gordijnen/tuin when true;
- cost factors instead of fake prices;
- proof/trust before CTA;
- FAQ about bezemschoon opleveren, bruikbare spullen, spoed, aanwezigheid, keys, and service area.

## MoFu Page

1. H1 around the problem or solution category.
2. What the reader is trying to solve.
3. Why common approaches fall short.
4. Criteria for a better solution.
5. Examples or use cases.
6. Objections and trade-offs.
7. Next step for evaluation.

## BoFu Page

1. H1 around decision-ready offer.
2. Who this is for.
3. What the offer includes.
4. Why choose this provider or approach.
5. Proof, process, and risk reduction.
6. Pricing or investment factors if available.
7. FAQ about starting, fit, and outcomes.
8. CTA.
